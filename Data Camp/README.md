
# Data Camp: Artificial Intelligence

A collection of certifications focused on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and AI Ethics.

### Completed Courses:
* **AI Ethics** – Foundations of ethical AI development and deployment.
* **Generative AI Concepts** – Core principles of LLMs and generative models.
* **Supervised Learning with scikit-learn** – Implementing predictive models in Python.
* **Unsupervised Learning in Python** – Clustering and dimensionality reduction.
* **Introduction to Deep Learning with PyTorch** – Building neural networks using PyTorch.
* **Reinforcement Learning with Gymnasium in Python** – Training agents in simulated environments.
